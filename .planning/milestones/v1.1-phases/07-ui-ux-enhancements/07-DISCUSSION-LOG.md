## Typography & HTML Formatting Consistency

| Option | Description | Selected |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| Bullet points (•) and bold subject | Bullet points for list items (•) with bold subject text: e.g., '• <b>Alex</b>: $50.00 (Dinner)'. Itinerary items use category emoji prefixing: e.g., '🏠 <b>Accommodation:</b> Hotel Hilton (Jul 12 - Jul 15)' | ✓ |
| Dashes (-) and italic subject | Standardized dashes for list items (-) with italic subject text: e.g., '- <i>Alex</i>: $50.00 (Dinner)'. Itinerary items use plain bullet points with category emoji. | |

**User's choice:** Bullet points (•) and bold subject text.
**Notes:** Decided on bold subject text to improve visibility and ease of scanning list items.
